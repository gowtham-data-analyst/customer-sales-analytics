# Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profit margins, and service trends using business intelligence and data visualization techniques.

---

## Dashboard Overview

This dashboard provides a complete sales analytics solution with insights into:

- Sales trends over time
- Customer acquisition and retention
- Department-wise margins
- Service performance analysis
- City-wise sales contribution
- Top customer analysis

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Trend Analysis
- Monthly sales tracking for 2023 and 2024
- Trend comparison across years

### Customer Source Analysis
- Sales generated through:
  - Ads
  - Organic
  - Walk-in customers

### Sales by City
- City-level contribution analysis:
  - Dubai
  - Abu Dhabi
  - Ajman
  - Al Ain
  - Sharjah

### Service Performance
- Sales comparison by service type:
  - Ads
  - App Development
  - SEO
  - Social Media
  - Web Development

### Department Margin Analysis
- Margin contribution by:
  - Copywriting
  - Design
  - Marketing

### New vs Repeat Sales
- Comparison of customer retention and acquisition performance

### Top Customers
- Identification of highest-value customers
